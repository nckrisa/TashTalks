Why Tash Talks?

Hello everyone! I'm Tash and I've recently found a passion for beauty, fashion, and all things lifestyle. I wanted to create this website as a means for young women to discover brands and products that might be useful to them and share my own personal opinions on them. In addition, I want to create an enviornment where all opinions are shared so other content creators will be featured on this website as well. I hope you enjoy!
